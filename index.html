About Me

My name is Amberly and I am a full stack web developer. I studied math and art, which are my two passions in life. To me, there are only a few things that are more satisfying than solving a complex problem. In my spare time, I enjoy practicing my makeup skills, playing video games, and hanging out with my family. I am a great lover of music as well, and I sang in my college choir for four years.

As a focused and driven individual, I am passionate about growing my skills and producing high quality work. My technical and artistic background sets me in a unique position to navigate between the language and expectations of engineers and designers. I am interested in how technology and design intersect and using my skills in both areas to create meaningful products. I am a fast learner and rise to any challenges that come my way.
