About Me

My name is Amberly and I am a full stack web developer. I studied math and art, which are my two passions in life. To me, there are only a few things that are more satisfying than solving a complex problem. In my spare time, I enjoy practicing my makeup skills, playing video games, and hanging out with my family. I am a great lover of music as well, and I sang in my college choir for four years.

As a focused and driven individual, I am passionate about growing my skills and producing high quality work. My technical and artistic background sets me in a unique position to navigate between the language and expectations of engineers and designers. I am interested in how technology and design intersect and using my skills in both areas to create meaningful products. I am a fast learner and rise to any challenges that come my way.

Message

Message is an ecommerce website that sells bouquets and plants based on the language of flowers. Visitors of the site can take a personality quiz for fun and browse the shop. If they want to write a review or buy products, they will be invited to log in or sign up for an account. After they have logged in, users are free to browse the site with ease and also view their profile to see past orders and reviews.

Oasis

Oasis is a travel community application where users can share tips about their favorite locations around the world. Oasis was created by Brandon Castillo, Karma Drukpa, Amberly Wang and Michael Claus. Users who are logged in can see their profile and their posts. They can edit their profile, create new posts, edit posts, and delete posts. Users can also see different cities and the posts for each specific city.

PlantPal

PlantPal is an app designed to simplify plant owners' lives and help them keep their plants alive. Users can sign up and add which plants they have from a designated list. Once they add their plants, it will automatically begin calculating when the plants' next water day is. When you have exceed the amount of time between waterings, the tracker will let you know and you can reset the counter after you water your plant. This project was created by Ghenet AndreMichael, Amberly Wang, and Michael Claus.

The Language of Flowers

This Language of Flowers dictionary allows you to search a database of flowers and plants by name or by meaning. This dictionary uses a flexible search bar that will output results as you type. Visitors can click on the flower or plant in the results portion to view an more details including an image and a short description. This dictionary utilizes a self made API with two hundred and seventy one flowers and plants.
